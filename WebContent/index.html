About Us

Pawssible is a website for street dog adoption , at pawssible you can easily find and adopt a perfect pet for your home.

Pawssible is an initative by three engineering students, it was started with a dream to provide shelter to every dog in India. There are over 40 Million street dogs with no access to food and shelter in India and our aim is find a sweet home for all such needy dogs through your help and support.

Frequently Asked Questions

We are dedicated to providing a seamless experience to all our users. To help guide you through, we have compiled a list of frequently asked questions and provided answers below.

  • Why one should adopt a dog?

    Did you know that over 2000 people per hour in India run a search looking to adopt a pet? Pet adoption is becoming the preferred way to find a new pet. Adoption will always be more convenient than buying a puppy for sale from a pet shop. Pet adoption brings less stress and more savings! So what are you waiting for? Go find that perfect pet for home

  • What is the fee for dog adoption?

    No, there is no fee for pet adoption on Pawssible. However, if you adopt from a different city pet owner/rescuer can ask for travel charges.

  • Can we return the adopted pet?

    We understand it can be hard to get an adjusted pet in the new home and vice versa, as long as your reason for returning is reasonable, you'll be welcome to put it up for adoption again.
